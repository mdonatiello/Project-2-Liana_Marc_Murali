Project Description

This project analyzed 2075 records on transportation construction projects in New York State. The specific dataset was "NYS Transportation Projects in Your Neighborhood" from the Open NY Dataset. CSV versions of the data were accessed from Kaggle.com at: "https://www.kaggle.com/new-york-state/nys-transportation-projects-in-your-neighborhood"

Insight!

The majority of funding in each region came from state sources followed by federal funding at roughly the same proportion to state funding in each region. Local funding was significantly less than state and federal funding in all regions. Long Island and Hudson Valley were numbers 1 and 2 respectively in terms of total funding..
